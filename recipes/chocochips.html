Chocolate Chip Cookies

Home Description

These classic chocolate chip cookies are soft in the center, chewy around the edges, and loaded with melty chocolate chips. Perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients

The ingredients include:

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • Â½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water and add to the batter along with salt.
  5. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  6. Bake for about 10 minutes, or until edges are nicely browned.
  7. Cool slightly before transferring to a wire rack. Enjoy warm or store for later!
